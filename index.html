About us

As a team of 4 students, we want the football community to become better. What sets us apart is our dedication and expertise within football as well as mental health. We value our customers above all else. Your satisfaction is our priority!

Mind the Ball is a brand that aims to connect and support football players within the field. Our website consists of tips and exercises to train your brain and deal with setbacks and pressure. Check it out now!

Our Vision

In a world where team players in sports constantly experience pressure from supporters, team coaches, and other players, we believe a strong resilient mind is as important as sports skills, because only then players will be able to perform at their best
